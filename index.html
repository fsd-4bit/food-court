COMPLETO, CHILE

Its unique natural barriers—the Pacific Ocean to the west, the Andes Mountains to the east and the Atacama Desert to the north—help protect vulnerable avocado trees from pests and disease. To sample a Chilean avocado staple, order a completo.

MEZE, MIDDLE EAST

Can’t decide what to eat off the menu? Order meze (also spelled mezze). It’s this part of the world’s version of Spanish tapas. Depending on where you are, meze could include a variety of local dishes that may include labne (strained yogurt cheese), baba ghanoush (seasoned diced eggplant).
